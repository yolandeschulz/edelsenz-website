Food & Beverage

Functional, trend-forward beverages built on our proprietary nutrition technologies.

Our Food & Beverage division develops functional, trend-forward drinks — with a particular focus on women’s wellness. From creatine and protein waters to next-generation wellness beverages, products are built on our proprietary nutrition platforms and SA-legislation-aligned claims.

What we do

Our offering

  • ✓Women’s-wellness positioning — cycle-aware, made for her biology.
  • ✓Novel functional ingredients — peanut protein, Peptan® collagen, oral thin-strip actives.
  • ✓Compliant claims aligned to South African legislation.
  • ✓End-to-end manufacturing, blending and co-packing.
