Our story

Built to make a difference in food

EdelSenz began with a long-held dream. After more than 18 years in corporate executive regional roles — learning what it takes to be globally relevant while delivering local, in-market solutions across Europe, the Middle East and Africa — founder Yolande Schulz set out to build a business that would make a real difference in FMCG.

Formed in 2025, EdelSenz brought together shareholders and a team of experts who shared one vision: to make a difference in food in Africa, offering customers innovative, competitively priced solutions and products. That ambition still sits at our core.

EdelFrom Edelweiss — honouring our founders’ European roots and global outlook.
SenzFrom African language roots — built in South Africa, with real local relevance.

Vision & mission

Vision

To become a trusted integrated innovation and execution partner for food, beverage and selected personal-care businesses — helping customers move from idea to shelf with technical excellence, practical execution and responsible growth.

Mission

To deliver science-backed, high-quality nutritional, functional and product solutions that improve public health, enhance product performance and support sustainable, traceable supply chains.
