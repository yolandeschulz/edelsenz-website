Milling & Baking

Heritage milling and modern bakery solutions, plus tailored vitamin & mineral premix blends.

From farm-harvested to traditionally farm-milled, our Milling & Baking division covers heritage maize milling, modern bakery mixes and the vitamin & mineral premixes used to fortify staples and finished goods.

What we do

Our offering

  • ✓Maize milling — unbleached, roller-mill maize meal from locally farmed and milled grain.
  • ✓Bakery solutions & mixes — mielie bread mix, flapjack and pancake mixes and more.
  • ✓Vitamin & mineral premixes for the fortification of staples and finished products.
  • ✓Technical & application support from formulation through to scale-up.
Who we serve

The customers we partner with

Bakeries, millers, staple-food producers and brands fortifying their products for better public-health outcomes.

How we create value

Why it works

Locally farmed and milled inputs mean a stable supply, while our technical team handles fortification, formulation and compliance — so quality and nutrition are built in, not bolted on.
