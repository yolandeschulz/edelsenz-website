Pantry & Home

Farm-to-shelf pantry ranges built from seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients.

Made from seasonal farm ingredients, our Pantry & Home division turns estate and farm produce into shelf-ready ranges — the model we built with Boschendal’s Farm Kitchen Range, from savoury and sweet pantry lines to maize, single-farm ingredients and home.

What we do

Our offering

  • ✓Farm Kitchen — savoury: artisanal pasta and pasta sauces (slow-cooked grass-fed beef bolognese).
  • ✓Farm Kitchen — sweet: ice-cream dessert-sauce trios and fruit preserves.
  • ✓Breakfast & snacking: granola, granola bars and a variety of rusks.
  • ✓Maize & single-farm ingredients: mielie bread mix, maize meal, raw pecans, extra-virgin cold-pressed olive oil.
  • ✓Body & Home: hand-crafted candles, diffusers and a natural skincare range.
Who we serve

The customers we partner with

Estates, farm brands and retailers who want a farm-to-shelf range that carries their story and quality.

How we create value

Why it works

We replicate estate SKUs, develop seasonal farm-led products, and handle compliance, labelling and logistics — so you keep brand control while EdelSenz handles operational execution.
